# Summary Report: "FCAJ Tech Sharing & Demo Day - 27/06/2026"

### Event Objectives

- Present real-world projects and advanced technology solutions developed by community members.
- Introduce cloud operations automation, AI Voice Agents, intelligent DevOps tools, and secure connectivity solutions.
- Discuss challenges of deploying AI at an enterprise scale and master Multi-Agent reasoning frameworks.

### Speakers & Topics

1. **Steve Tran** - Topic: *Deep Response Engine: From Detection to Autonomous Resolution*
2. **Nghia Danh - Trung Vu - Kiet Tran** - Topic: *Voice Agents: Building Human-Like AI Conversations at Scale*
3. **Nguyen Nguyen - Bao Phan** - Topic: *AWS DevOps Agent: Your Always-Available Operations Teammate*
4. **Truong Tran - Minh Anh** - Topic: *AI-Powered Productivity: Workforce Planning For Enterprise*
5. **Toan Nguyen - Nghi Danh** - Topic: *Building Secure Private MCP Connection with Amazon Quick*

### Key Highlights from Presentations

#### 1. Deep Response Engine: From Detection to Autonomous Resolution
**Speaker:** Steve Tran

- **The complexity wall in modern cloud operations:** Analyzed the difficulties of managing vast amounts of alerts within complex microservices.
- **Shift from alert-driven to action-driven systems:** Shifting mindset from merely receiving warnings to deploying automated remediation actions.
- **Deep Response Engine architecture overview:** Designing automated pipelines to detect and resolve system incidents.
- **Live demo of autonomous incident response:** Visualized how the engine automatically detects service faults and recovers without engineering intervention.
- **Business impact:** Drastically reducing operational costs and ensuring zero-downtime operations.

#### 2. Voice Agents: Building Human-Like AI Conversations at Scale
**Speakers:** Nghia Danh - Trung Vu - Kiet Tran

- **Evolution from IVR and chatbots to AI voice agents:** Analyzed the leap in AI technology that enables more natural human-like voice conversations.
- **Key challenges:** Addressing latency optimization, speech-to-text accuracy, and natural conversational flow.
- **Amazon Nova Sonic integration:** Exploiting AWS's next-generation speech-to-speech foundation model.
- **System architecture:** Combining telephony networks, streaming pipelines, Amazon Bedrock, and MCP (Model Context Protocol) tools.
- **Enterprise use cases & Live Demo:** A practical demo of an AI voice agent interacting smoothly with customers over a call.

#### 3. AWS DevOps Agent: Your Always-Available Operations Teammate
**Speakers:** Nguyen Nguyen - Bao Phan

- **Overview of AWS DevOps Agent:** A 24/7 intelligent virtual AI assistant supporting technical teams.
- **Reducing MTTD and MTTR with AI-driven operations:** Accelerating Mean Time to Detection (MTTD) and Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR) using AI-assisted operations.
- **Supporting multi-cloud and hybrid environments:** Porting operational capabilities flexibly across multi-cloud and on-premises hybrid infrastructures.
- **Bedrock AgentCore and multi-agent reasoning approach:** Collaborating multiple specialized AI Agents to solve complex enterprise problems.
- **Real-world use cases & ECS demo walkthrough:** A step-by-step walkthrough of the Agent detecting and fixing container issues on Amazon ECS.

#### 4. AI-Powered Productivity: Workforce Planning For Enterprise
**Speakers:** Truong Tran - Minh Anh

- **HR transformation challenges in modern enterprises:** Solving talent management and workforce optimization problems at scale.
- **Overview of Amazon Q and its HR capabilities:** How AI interacts with internal documents to automate HR tasks intelligently.
- **Accelerating HR operations with automation:** Minimizing processing times for paperwork, approval workflows, and employee engagement.
- **Workforce analytics and data-driven insights:** Unearthing predictions and insights from HR databases.
- **Strategic workforce planning for enterprise decision-making:** Assisting executives in optimizing workforce allocation using long-term predictive data.

#### 5. Building Secure Private MCP Connection with Amazon Quick
**Speakers:** Toan Nguyen - Nghi Danh

- **Introduction to Amazon Quick as an AI assistant platform:** AWS's secure enterprise-grade AI assistant platform.
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol) and its role in extensibility:** The vital role of MCP in securely connecting AI models with external data sources.
- **Security challenges in MCP-based integrations:** The risks of data leakage when routing MCP requests over public networks.
- **Configuring Amazon Quick VPC private connectivity:** Establishing completely isolated internal network configurations for Amazon Quick to communicate with MCP servers.
- **Demo and real-world implementation insights:** Step-by-step guidance on setting up and securing sensitive enterprise data.

- **On Cloud Security:** Learned how to establish private cloud connections (VPC Endpoints) to protect sensitive AI message transmissions.

- **VPC Security Design:** Implement private connection topologies using VPC Endpoints to protect sensitive data transfers.
